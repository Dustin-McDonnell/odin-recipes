Pineapple Pizza

Description

Pineapple pizzas are one of the highest-healing foods in RuneScape, healing 22 Hitpoints (11 per bite). Two bites of the same pizza can be eaten in quick succession, similar to a piece of regular food and a Karambwan. It can be made at level 65 Cooking by adding pineapple chunks or pineapple rings to a cooked plain pizza, granting 45 Cooking experience (for a total of 189 experience from start to finish).

Ingredients

  • Pot of Flour
  • Bucket of Water
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Pineapple Ring

Steps

  1. Make a pizza base by using the bucket of water on the pot of flour.
  2. Add a tomato to the pizza base to create an incomplete pizza.
  3. Add a cheese to the incomplete pizza to create an uncooked pizza.
  4. Use the uncooked pizza on a range to create the plain pizza (or, if burnt, a burnt pizza).
  5. Add a pineapple ring to the plain pizza, to create a finihed Pineapple Pizza
